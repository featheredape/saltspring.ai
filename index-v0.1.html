About the Workshop

This summer, Salt Spring Island becomes a launchpad for a creative AI community across the Gulf Islands and surrounding areas. Over five immersive days, participants will explore four key areas of AI application: visualization, motion, sound, and data, guided by presenters from diverse creative and technical backgrounds.


The week caps off with a curated public art show featuring provocative creative works exploring AI and agency. Whether you're a programmer, artist, musician, designer, researcher, or simply curious, this is your invitation to shape how AI serves our community.


Space is limited and participation requires a short application. We're looking for presenters and participants from any creative or technical background.

Workshop Tracks

Four Paths of Exploration

Each track runs as a focused deep-dive with hands-on sessions, collaborative projects, and creative challenges.

Visualization

From data storytelling to generative imagery. Explore how AI transforms raw information into compelling visual narratives and art.

Motion

Animation, video, and embodied AI. Discover tools that bring movement and life to creative projects, from film to robotics.

Sound

Music generation, audio synthesis, and sonic landscapes. Use AI to compose, remix, and reimagine what sound can be.

Data

Local datasets, community insights, and practical intelligence. Learn how AI can help solve real problems on the island and beyond.
